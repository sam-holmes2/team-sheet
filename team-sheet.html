?  HOW TEAM-SHEET WORKS
Setup with your AI
team-sheet is designed for ongoing IFS journalling. Your AI reads your sessions and updates your inner system map.
1
Download instructions.md from the same folder as team-sheet.html. This tells your AI how the app works and what JSON to produce.
2
Create a project in your AI. Upload instructions.md to the project knowledge so it's always attached to every session.
3
Start a session. Try: "Here's what's been present lately..." or use a Session Starter on the Journal tab.
4
At the end, ask your AI: "Update my data.json" and copy the output.

Your AI infers which parts were present, how relationships shifted, and whether any Self-led moments occurred. Lock any field to protect your own writing from being overwritten.

↑  Import
Load your data into team-sheet after a session, or restore a backup from another device.
1
Open Import:
2
Paste your data.json into the text area and hit Apply. Or use the file picker to load directly from disk.
3
The app updates instantly. New parts appear on the Map. Session entries appear in the Journal tab. Balance history grows over time.

Lock icons protect individual fields from being overwritten. Click ⊙ on any field to protect it, so your own writing survives future imports.

↓  Export
Your data lives in browser storage. Export regularly so you have a backup and so your AI stays up to date.
1
Export your data:
2
Store it somewhere safe. data.json contains your private journal and inner system map. Treat it like a diary.
3
Before a new session, paste this JSON into your AI project to replace the old data.json. Your AI then has your latest state.

Set a password in Security to encrypt your data at rest in the browser. Good practice if you share a device or browser profile.

⚙  SECURITY

⚠ Your data is stored only in this browser. Nothing leaves your machine unless you send it. If you journal with an AI, that provider may store what you share with them. Use Firefox if possible; Chrome and Edge share storage across local files. A password encrypts your data at rest in the browser.

Password Protection

When set, all local data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM. Use a long random password from a password manager for strongest protection.

⚠ If you forget your password, your data cannot be recovered. Keep an export of your data.json as a backup.
